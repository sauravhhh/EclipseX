About Eclipses

Solar Eclipse

Occurs when the Moon passes between Earth and the Sun, temporarily blocking the Sun's light. Types include Total, Partial, and Annular eclipses.

Lunar Eclipse

Happens when Earth passes between the Sun and Moon, casting Earth's shadow on the Moon. Safe to view with the naked eye.
